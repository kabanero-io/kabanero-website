Install Management CLI

Once Kabanero Foundation is installed, activate a stack or create a new stack for your team. Configure your tools and set up your pipelines for your team. Kabanero Foundation provides governance over the set of stacks available to your development team to use, empowering to create applications quickly while complying with company policies.
